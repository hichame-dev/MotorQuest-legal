1. Introduction

Welcome to MotorQuest ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application MotorQuest (the "App").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Register for an account
  • Update your profile
  • Use features of the App

This includes:

  • Email address - Required for account creation and authentication
  • Display name - Your chosen username visible to other users
  • Profile photo - Optional, uploaded by you

2.2 Location Data

We collect location data to provide our core services:

  • Precise GPS location - Used during active ride tracking to record your motorcycle routes
  • Background location - Used to continue tracking when the screen is off (only when you start a ride)
Important: Location data is only collected when you actively start a ride. We do not track your location passively.

2.3 Ride and Usage Data

  • Ride statistics - Distance, duration, speed, routes taken
  • Lean angle data - Collected from device sensors during rides
  • App usage - Features used, screens visited (anonymized analytics)

2.4 Device Information

  • Device type and model - For app optimization
  • Operating system version - For compatibility
  • Unique device identifiers - For authentication and security

3. How We Use Your Information

Purpose Data Used
Provide ride tracking services GPS location, device sensors
Create and manage your account Email, name, profile photo
Display your statistics and achievements Ride data, lean angles
Show routes near you Approximate location
Improve the App Anonymized usage data

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

4.1 Firebase (Google)

  • Purpose: Authentication, cloud storage, crash reporting
  • Data shared: Email, device ID, crash logs
  • Privacy Policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

4.2 Mapbox

  • Purpose: Map display, route rendering
  • Data shared: Location data (when viewing maps)
  • Privacy Policy: mapbox.com/legal/privacy

4.3 MongoDB Atlas

  • Purpose: Database hosting
  • Data shared: All user data (encrypted in transit and at rest)
  • Privacy Policy: mongodb.com/legal/privacy-policy

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Storage

Your data is stored on:

  • MongoDB Atlas - Cloud database (encrypted)
  • Firebase - Authentication and file storage
  • Your device - Local cache for offline access

5.2 Security Measures

We implement:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transfers
  • AES-256 encryption for sensitive local data
  • Secure token storage using device keychain
  • Certificate pinning to prevent man-in-the-middle attacks
  • Regular security audits

5.3 Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Ride history: Retained until you delete individual rides or your account
  • Location data: Only stored as part of completed rides

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights:

6.1 Right to Access

You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

6.2 Right to Rectification

You can update or correct your personal information at any time through the App settings.

6.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data.

6.4 Right to Data Portability

You can request your data in a portable format (GPX for rides).

6.5 Right to Restrict Processing

You can ask us to limit how we use your data.

6.6 Right to Object

You can object to certain types of data processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: privacy@motorquest.app

7. Children's Privacy

MotorQuest is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal data
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your data for targeted advertising

We MAY share data:

  • With your consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights or safety

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence, including the United States (Firebase, MongoDB Atlas). We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the App
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Sending you a notification for significant changes
